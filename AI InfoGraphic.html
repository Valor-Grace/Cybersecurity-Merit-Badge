7Ô∏è‚É£ Practical Application (Do ONE)

Now it's time to get hands-on! Choose one of these two practical options to apply what you've learned. Both require your counselor's approval first.

Option A: AI Project

Choose a project based on personal interest or a community need. You'll create a plan (objectives, data, ethics), implement it using AI tools, and share your experience.

Option B: Teach a Lesson

Design a short lesson plan on AI and teach it to your patrol. Include an AI-generated explanation, examples, and an interactive demo. Share your teaching experience.

8Ô∏è‚É£ Career Exploration (Do ONE)

Finally, look to the future. Explore career paths that use AI and automation skills by choosing one of these two options.

Option A: Career Research

Identify 3 AI careers. Pick one and do a deep dive: research the training, education, costs, salary, and job outlook. Discuss your findings.

Option B: Interview a Professional

Interview an AI or automation professional. Ask about their work, challenges, and vision for the future, as well as the training and education required.
